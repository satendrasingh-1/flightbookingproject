Spirit Airlines

Spirited Airlines is a renowned United States-based airline known for its low cost, extensive network, exceptional service, and commitment to passenger satisfaction. With attractive pricing, diverse snacking options, and a reliable reputation, Spirited Airlines is a top choice for travellers worldwide.

Operating for several decades, Spirited Airlines serves over 60 destinations across the United States, ensuring seamless connections between cities and countries. Major routes include Atlanta to Fort Lauderdale, Orlando to San Juan, and Las Vegas to Chicago. International destinations like Colombia, Costa Rica, and Cuba are also easily accessible. With over 500 daily flights, Spirited Airlines connects passengers to about 20 countries.

Luggage Details

Passengers traveling with Spirited Airlines can carry one personal item free of charge. Checked baggage fees vary based on destination. Due to the low-ticket cost, fares typically do not include checked bags. The maximum weight permitted for checked baggage is generally 40 pounds, above which an extra charge is required.

Cabin Details

Spirit Airlines Free Spirit Loyalty Program

The Free Spirit loyalty program allows passengers to earn miles for every purchase with Spirited Airlines or its selected partners. Miles can be redeemed for flight seats and partner products and services. The Free Spirit Gold Status permits passengers to carry free checked and carry-on bags and offers free onboard drinks and snacks.
