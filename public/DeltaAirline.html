Delta Airlines

Delta Airlines is a well-established American airline renowned for its extensive global network, exceptional customer service, and dedication to passenger satisfaction. With a wide range of cabin classes, diverse dining options, and a reputation for reliability, Delta Airlines is a top choice for travelers worldwide.

Operating for over 90 years, Delta Airlines serves more than 325 destinations across six continents, ensuring seamless connections between cities and countries. Popular routes include New York City to Los Angeles, Atlanta to Chicago, and Boston to San Francisco. Additionally, Delta Airlines provides access to international destinations like Paris, London, Tokyo, and Sydney. With over 5,400 daily flights, Delta Airlines connects passengers to more than 300 destinations in over 50 countries.

Luggage Details

Passengers traveling with Delta Airlines are allowed free carry-on bags and personal items. Checked baggage fees vary depending on the fare class and destination. Economy fares generally do not include checked bags, while Main Cabin fares typically include one free checked bag weighing up to 50 pounds. First-Class fares often allow two free checked bags, each weighing up to 70 pounds, on domestic US flights.

Cabin Details

Delta Airlines offers a range of cabin classes to cater to different types of travelers. First Class passengers enjoy spacious seats and ample legroom, while Delta Comfort+ customers receive complimentary food and drinks. All cabins feature personal entertainment displays with a wide range of movies and TV shows, as well as USB outlets for charging electronic devices.

Delta's SkyMiles Loyalty Program

Delta's SkyMiles loyalty program allows Main Cabin travelers to earn points for flying with Delta or its partner airlines, as well as for everyday expenses. These points can be redeemed for several rewards, including flight upgrades, access to Delta Sky Club lounges, and premium drinks. Members can also utilize their never-expiring miles to travel to over 1,000 destinations. Medallion Status offers additional benefits such as unlimited upgrades to First Class and Delta Comfort+, extra miles at each tier, and reduced baggage fees. Delta SkyMiles members can earn miles through the American Express Delta SkyMiles Credit Card and with partner airlines and brands. The Miles + Cash option is available for members to purchase their next flight.
