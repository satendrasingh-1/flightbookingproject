Air Canada Airlines

Air Canada Airlines is a renowned Canadian airline known for its extensive network, exceptional customer service, and commitment to passenger satisfaction. With various cabin classes, diverse dining options, and a reputation for reliability, Air Canada is a popular choice for travelers worldwide.

Operating for several decades, Air Canada serves over 200 destinations across six continents, facilitating seamless connections between cities and countries. Major routes include Toronto to Vancouver, Montreal to Calgary, and Ottawa to Edmonton. International destinations like London, Paris, Tokyo, and Sydney are also accessible. With over 1,500 daily flights, Air Canada connects passengers to more than 50 countries.

Luggage Details

Passengers are allowed to bring carry-on bags and personal items free of charge. Checked baggage fees vary based on fare class and destination. Economy fares usually do not include checked bags, while Premium Economy and Business Class fares often include at least one checked bag. The maximum weight permitted for checked baggage is typically 50 pounds.

Cabin Details

Air Canada offers a range of cabin classes tailored to different travelers. Economy provides comfortable seating and in-flight entertainment, while Premium Economy offers extra legroom and enhanced dining. Business Class and Signature Class offer premium services such as lie-flat beds and exclusive lounge access.

Air Canada Altitude Loyalty Program

The Altitude loyalty program allows passengers to earn points, called Altitude Qualifying Miles (AQMs), for flying with Air Canada or its partners. Points can be redeemed for flight upgrades, lounge access, and more. Different tiers offer benefits like bonus miles and priority services. Points can also be earned through credit cards and shopping with program partners.
